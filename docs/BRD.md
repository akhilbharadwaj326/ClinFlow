# Business Requirements Document (BRD) - ClinFlow

## 1. Executive Summary
Small clinics and independent healthcare providers often rely on paper notes, handwritten documents, and messaging applications (like Telegram) to capture patient information. While convenient, this fragmented approach makes patient records difficult to search, understand, and reuse. ClinFlow is a continuity-of-care platform that transforms these scattered clinical records into a structured, trustworthy, patient-centric history. 

## 2. Business Objectives
- **Improve Continuity of Care:** Enable any doctor in a clinic to understand a patient's case within minutes and continue care safely.
- **Reduce Clinical Errors:** Minimize risks associated with missing information, redundant questioning, and duplicate tests.
- **Standardize Documentation:** Elevate documentation quality across the clinic without forcing doctors into expensive and complex traditional EHR systems.

## 3. Target Audience & Personas
- **Primary Persona (Core User):** Reviewing Doctor (e.g., Dr. Priya) - Needs to quickly understand a patient's history and ensure no critical context is missed before continuing treatment.
- **Secondary Persona (Note Creator):** Treating Doctor (e.g., Dr. Rajesh) - Wants to document encounters quickly without administrative burden while keeping notes useful for others.
- **Operational Persona:** Clinic Assistant (e.g., Anita) - Needs an organized system to collect, upload, and search patient files and messages.
- **Buyer Persona:** Clinic Owner / Lead Physician (e.g., Dr. Mehta) - Wants to improve clinic efficiency and documentation standards without investing in a costly full-scale EHR.

## 4. Value Proposition
Most AI healthcare tools sell documentation speed (writing notes faster). ClinFlow sells **continuity of care** and **understanding**. The platform does not invent facts; it serves as a clinical understanding layer that organizes notes, highlights uncertainty, and surfaces missing details, preserving trust and accuracy.

## 5. Pricing and Business Model
- **Target Market:** Small multi-specialty clinics (2–10 doctors).
- **Pricing Strategy:** Clinic Subscription model (e.g., per clinic workspace). ClinFlow solves a clinic-wide problem, so a shared workspace aligns with the value delivered.
- **Cost Sustainability:** Optimize AI usage (LLMs used selectively for structuring and normalization), while standard software handles storage, search, and access control.

## 6. Success Metrics (KPIs)
- Improve note completeness from 70% to 95%.
- Reduce clinical handoff misunderstandings by 40%.
- Decrease the time a reviewing doctor spends reconstructing patient history.
